About Me

Hello, I'm Thanavithyasswary Sankar and during this course I developed NomNom, an application inspired by SDG 2: Zero Hunger. The app helps people access food and donate surplus food to reduce waste. Building this app gave me valuable hands-on experience in Android development, along with many challenges that strengthened my problem-solving skills and prepared me for creating impactful applications in the future.
